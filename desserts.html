1. Maamoul

Maamoul is a common arab dessert made during holidays like Eid or Easter. It is a “cookie” filled with pistachio, walnuts, dates, etc. There are many different combinations of fillings for Mamool.

2. Kaak

Kaak is another arab dessert, similar to a cookie. It is also served during holidays.

3. Helbeh, also known as fenugreek cake

The cake is made with a combination of fenugreek seeds, semolina flour, sugar, and butter, which are mixed together to create a rich, dense cake with a distinct aroma and flavor.The fenugreek seeds give the cake a slightly bitter, nutty taste that is complemented by the sweetness of the sugar and the richness of the butter. The semolina flour gives the cake a crumbly texture that is similar to shortbread, and the overall result is a delicious and unique dessert that is sure to please any sweet tooth.
